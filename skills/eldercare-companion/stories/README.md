# Thu muc truyen cho ba

## Cach them truyen

1. Tao file text (.txt) trong thu muc nay
2. Dat ten: `01-ten-truyen.txt`, `02-ten-truyen.txt`
3. Format trong file:

```
# Ten Truyen

## Chuong 1: Ten chuong

Noi dung doan 1...

Noi dung doan 2...

## Chuong 2: Ten chuong

Noi dung...
```

4. He thong tu dong detect va them vao danh sach

## Goi y truyen cho ba

- Truyen co tich Viet Nam (Tam Cam, Su tich trau cau, Thanh Giong...)
- Truyen ngan dan gian mien Nam
- Truyen Phat giao (neu ba theo dao Phat)
- Hoi ky, lich su Viet Nam
- Truyen tinh cam nhe nhang

## Luu y

- Moi doan nen ngan (200-300 tu) de TTS doc vua du
- Ngon ngu don gian, de hieu
- Tranh noi dung buon hoac bao luc
- Dung dau cham (.) cuoi cau de TTS ngat dung cho
